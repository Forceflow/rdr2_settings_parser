RDR2 settings.xml Parser (a work in progress!)

Tired of taking screenshots of your settings to share in discussions online?

Paste the contents of your RDR2 settings file (found at /Users/Username/Documents/Rockstar Games/Red Dead Redemption 2/Settings/system.xml) in the left area,
hit the button, and a nicely formatted settings block will appear on the right, ready to copy paste into your favorite comment/discussion.

For feedback, use this reddit thread or the Github repository .

Currently works for the basic (non-locked) settings, except for globillum, which I still need to find :p

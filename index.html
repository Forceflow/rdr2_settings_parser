Red Dead Redemption 2 system.xml Settings Parser v0.4

Red Dead Redemption 2 for PC has a lot of graphics settings. The presets are system-dependent. Some of the RDR2 updates annoyingly reset your settings.
Taking screenshots of all your settings is clunky, so why not parse that settings file into a nice text block?

Paste the contents of your RDR2 settings file (found at (My Documents)/Rockstar Games/Red Dead Redemption 2/Settings/system.xml) in the left area
and a nicely formatted settings block will appear on the right, ready to copy/paste into your favorite online discussion.

  • For feedback: Reddit, Github, GTAForums or @jbaert.
  • Good threads with settings and discussion: Guru3D, ResetEra, Reddit.
  • The game only saves your settings to system.xml on succesful exit.
  • If you like this, buy me a coffee more games.


Input: system.xml

Output: Parsed settings
